We are Partial History

We are a nimble, multi-disciplinary creative studio with more than a decade of experience in web design/development and digital marketing.

I'm Jessica, the human behind Partial History Creative Studio. I'm a millennial who took a very millennial career path and accidentally assembled a killer set of design, marketing, and tech skills.

I've got more than a decade of expertise in web design/development and digital marketing. My last in-house job was with Huge, a global design and technology powerhouse, where I was a User Interface Engineer on the Google account.

As a marketer, I've worked for agencies such as Saatchi & Saatchi, Wunderman, Track DDB, and The JAR Group, and I've managed brands including Procter and Gamble, Unilever, Kraft, Scotiabank, and Toyota.

As a freelancer, I've built sites and brands for marketing agencies, wellness practitioners, and boutique clothing brands.

What does all this mean for you? It means that I know how to identify your audience and speak to them. I know how to build and customize a wide variety of websites, so you'll never be stuck with a template that isn't the right fit. But most of all? It means that I have a whole assortment of tools under my belt for design, marketing, and tech, and you'll be able to access every single one of them.
